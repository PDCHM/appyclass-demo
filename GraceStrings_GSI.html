INSTITUTION FAQs
Managing Classes
Add, delete, rename, capacity, schedules
How do I add a new class?
Go to Classes in the sidebar and tap + New in the top right. Fill in the class name, level, schedule and assign a trainer. The class will appear immediately in your Timetable and on your trainers' dashboards.
How do I delete or archive a class?
Open the class from Classes, scroll to Settings tab, and choose Archive class or Delete. Archiving keeps all historical data (attendance, recaps, assessments) while hiding the class from active view. Deletion is permanent — we recommend archiving instead.
Can I change the trainer assigned to a class?
Yes. Open the class → Settings tab → change the Assigned Trainer dropdown. The new trainer immediately sees the class in their dashboard. Historical data stays attributed to the previous trainer.
How do I set a maximum class size?
In Settings → Learner Access you can set a default max class size. You can also override it per class in the class Settings tab. When the limit is reached, new learners are waitlisted.
Can learners be in more than one class?
Yes. A learner can be enrolled in multiple classes simultaneously. Their progress and assessments are tracked separately per class. Billing is per learner per class.
Payouts & Payments
Release payouts, failed payments, refunds
When are trainer payouts processed?
Payouts are released by you manually from Payout Details — you can do this at any time during the month. The default payout schedule is monthly, but you can change it to bi-monthly or weekly in Settings → Payout Settings. Grace does not auto-release on your behalf without your approval.
Why does the payout amount look different from revenue?
Trainer payouts reflect their revenue share (default 70%) of their gross class earnings. The remaining 30% is the platform fee retained by GraceStrings. You can see the exact split breakdown per trainer in Payout Details → View details.
A trainer's bank transfer failed — what do I do?
If a transfer fails, it will appear as Failed in Payout History within 2 banking days. Ask the trainer to update their bank details in their Payout Details page. Once updated, re-release the payout from your admin panel. Contact GraceStrings support if the issue persists.
Can I issue a refund to a learner?
Refunds are initiated from the learner's profile. Go to Learners, find the learner, and select Issue refund. Refunds typically process within 5–10 business days. Partial refunds (e.g. prorated for classes missed) are also supported.
How does GraceStrings charge GraceStrings?
GraceStrings charges your saved payment method monthly for the Pro plan (S$29/mo) and any add-ons. This is separate from trainer payouts — your GraceStrings subscription is billed automatically. You can download invoices in My Subscription → Billing.
Can I change a trainer's revenue share percentage?
Yes. Go to Settings → Payout Settings to change the default for all new trainers. To change an existing trainer's share, open their trainer profile and override it there. Changes apply from the following payout period.
Learners
Enrolment, removing, transfers, access
How do I add a learner to GraceStrings?
Go to Learners → + Invite or send an invite link from the class Settings. Learners receive an email to create their account. Alternatively, enable Open Registration in Settings → Learner Access so anyone with the link can self-enrol.
How do I remove a learner from a class?
Open Classes → [Class] → Learners, find the learner, and tap Remove. They lose access to the class immediately. Their historical attendance and assessment data is retained for your records. If you want to remove them from GraceStrings entirely, go to Learners and delete their profile.
A learner says they can't log in — what should I do?
First check they're using the right email address (their GraceStrings login). Ask them to use Forgot password on the login screen. If that doesn't work, contact GraceStrings support with the learner's email and we'll investigate within 1 hour.
Can I move a learner from one class to another?
Yes — this is a class transfer. Go to the learner's profile, tap Transfer class, and select the new class. Grace AI's assessment record and progress data moves with them. You can also use the Promote tab in AI Assessment for learners Grace has flagged as ready to advance.
What happens when I hit my learner limit?
New enrolments are paused and learners are waitlisted. You'll see a warning in My Subscription. You can add extra learner seats (S$9/mo per 50 seats) without changing your plan, or upgrade to School (500 learner cap). Existing learners are not affected.
Trainers
Inviting, removing, permissions, rev share
How do I invite a new trainer?
Go to Classes → Invite another trainer (at the bottom) or Revenue → + Invite trainer. Enter their name, email, assign classes, and set a revenue share. They receive an invite email and get their own trainer dashboard once they accept.
How do I remove a trainer?
Go to the trainer's profile in Payout Details and select Remove trainer. Their classes are unassigned (you can reassign them to another trainer). Any pending payout for the current period is still due to them. Their historical data is retained.
Can a trainer see other trainers' classes or revenue?
No. Trainers only see their own assigned classes, learners, and earnings. Only admins (you) have access to the full institution overview across all trainers.
How many trainers can I have?
The Pro plan allows up to 5 trainers. School allows 20. Enterprise is unlimited. If you need more trainers before upgrading, contact support — we can temporarily increase the limit for an existing plan.
Technical Issues
Login problems, Zoom, app errors, data
The Zoom session link isn't working — what do I do?
Check that the Zoom integration is connected in Settings → Integrations. If it was working before, try disconnecting and reconnecting the Zoom account. Zoom outages are rare but check status.zoom.us for platform issues. As a fallback, use Google Meet — paste the link into your class broadcast.
Grace AI isn't generating assessments — what's wrong?
First check that video upload is enabled for the class in AI Assessment → By Class → [Class]. If enabled, check that the learner submitted a video (not just a note). Video processing takes 2–5 minutes. If the issue persists after 10 minutes, contact support with the learner's name and submission time.
Session recaps aren't sending to learners
Check the notification channels in the recap settings (email/in-app toggles). Also verify learner email addresses are correct in their profiles. Some email providers may filter GraceStrings emails to spam — ask learners to check and whitelist noreply@gracestrings.sg.
How do I export my institution's data?
Go to Settings → Danger Zone → Export all data. You'll receive a CSV and JSON export of all learners, attendance, revenue, assessments and sessions. The export is emailed to your admin email within 10 minutes.
The dashboard is showing wrong revenue figures
Revenue figures update every 15 minutes. If numbers still look wrong after a refresh, check if any refunds were issued recently (these reduce the monthly total). For persistent discrepancies, contact support with a specific month and the expected vs shown figures — we'll audit the payment records.

Grace AI
How it works, accuracy, configuration
How does Grace AI assess learners?
Grace analyses video or audio submissions from learners against the criteria you configure in AI Assessment → By Class. She scores each criterion (e.g. chord accuracy, timing, strumming pattern) and weights them according to your settings. Scores above your passing threshold are auto-approved if you've turned that on.
How accurate is Grace AI?
Grace currently operates at ~92% accuracy against trainer-reviewed scores across all GraceStrings assessments. The more you review and override, the better she calibrates to your specific standards. You can see your institution's accuracy score in AI Assessment → Activity.
Can I turn off Grace AI for specific classes?
Yes. In AI Assessment → By Class, select the class and toggle AI Assessment Active off. The class will revert to manual assessment only — trainers review submissions directly without Grace's pre-scoring.
Does Grace AI learn over time?
Yes. Every time you approve or override a Grace score, that signal trains her calibration for your institution specifically. After ~50 reviews, accuracy typically improves by 3–5%. Grace also updates her session recap and practice plan generation based on what you publish vs discard.
Subscription & Billing
Plans, invoices, cancellation, upgrades
How do I upgrade from Pro to School?
Go to My Subscription → Upgrade plan → School and tap the upgrade button. You'll be charged the prorated difference for the current billing period immediately. Your new limits (20 trainers, 500 learners, 50 GB) are active within minutes.
Can I cancel my GraceStrings subscription?
Yes, there's no lock-in. Go to Settings → Danger Zone or contact support. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. Your data is retained for 90 days after cancellation, during which you can export it.
Where can I download invoices?
Go to My Subscription → Subscription & Billing. The last 12 months of invoices are listed there as PDF downloads. For older invoices, contact support at +65 9123 4567 · WhatsApp.
My payment failed — what happens to my school's access?
After a failed payment, you have a 7-day grace period to update your payment method before access is restricted. Trainer and learner dashboards remain active during this time. You'll receive email reminders on days 1, 3 and 6. Update your card in My Subscription → Payment method → Update.
